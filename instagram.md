================================================================================
YAP - INSTAGRAM
================================================================================

Handle: @yapagency (oder @yap.fail)
Konzept: Notification-Screenshots als Content. Das Produkt erstellt seinen
eigenen Content. Jede Notification ist unique, kurz, lustig, visuell.


================================================================================
STRATEGIE
================================================================================

CONTENT-TYPEN:
- Posts: Beste Notification-Screenshots, clean auf schwarzem/weißem BG
- Stories: "Notification of the Day" — Polls welcher Agent härter war
- Reels: Notification-Compilations (Eskalation von Level 1 bis 5)
- UGC: "DM us your best notification" → Repost mit Credit

POSTING-FREQUENZ:
- 3-4 Posts/Woche (Notification Screenshots)
- Täglich 1 Story (Poll, Quiz, oder Behind-the-Scenes)
- 1-2 Reels/Woche (Eskalation-Compilations, Agent-Battles)

VISUELLER STIL:
- Lock-Screen-Screenshots, clean, kein Clutter
- Agent-Name + Avatar unten oder oben
- Schwarzer oder weißer Hintergrund
- Kein Logo-Spam, kein "Download now!"
- Der Content soll aussehen wie ein Meme, nicht wie eine Ad

BIO:
12 AI agents. 1 mission. 0 mercy.
Your mom, your ex, and 10 more vs. your to-do list.
iOS: [App Store link]

CAPTIONS:
- Kurz. 1-2 Sätze max. Agent-Voice oder Meta-Kommentar.
- Beispiel: "The Ex has been quiet today. That's worse."
- Beispiel: "Mom found out you skipped leg day."
- CTA nur gelegentlich: "Link in bio if you want this energy on your lock screen"

HASHTAGS (sparsam, 5-8 max):
#productivity #motivation #adhd #procrastination #ai #appstore
#guilttripbutmakeitproductive

  which one would make YOU clean faster? comment below.

SERIE 2: "Escalation Series"
1 Agent, 5 Notifications, Level 1 → Level 5.

  Beispiel: The Ex bei "Reply to that text" — von mild bis brutal.
  Bild: Carousel mit 5 Slides, jede Slide = 1 Notification.
  Slide 1 (mild): "You haven't replied yet. Interesting."
  Slide 2: "Seen at 2pm. It's 6pm. Cool."
  Slide 3: "This is very on brand for you."
  Slide 4: "Wow."
  Slide 5: "I'm not even surprised anymore."

  Caption:
  the escalation from "interesting" to "i'm not even surprised anymore" took 4 hours

  how far does your agent get before you give in? most people tap out at level 3.

  #escalation #theex #productivity #yapapp


SERIE 3: "Guess the Agent"
Notification ohne Agent-Name, Follower raten in Comments.

  Bild: iPhone Lock Screen, aber Agent-Name ist zensiert / verpixelt.
  Nur der Notification-Text ist sichtbar. Follower müssen raten
  welcher Agent das war.

  Caption:
  guess the agent. wrong answers only.

  (right answers also accepted. drop it in the comments.)

  #guesstheagent #yapapp #quiz


SERIE 4: "The Worst One"
Wöchentlich brutalste Notification, Follower voten.

  Bild: 4 Notifications von verschiedenen Agents, nummeriert 1-4.
  Poll in den Comments oder als Story-Poll.

  Caption:
  which one is the worst? vote 1-4.

  every friday we post the most unhinged notifications of the week. you decide the winner.

  this week's lineup is... rough.

  #theworstone #friday #yapapp #vote
